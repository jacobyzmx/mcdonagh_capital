About Us

McDonagh Capital is a New Zealand-based investment company with a long-term vision for real estate growth.

Founded by a driven individual with a passion for financial independence and property development, McDonagh Capital is currently focused on building its first property and laying the foundation for future investment opportunities.

While still in its early stages, the company is driven by integrity, strategy, and a commitment to long-term value — with eyes on residential and mixed-use properties across New Zealand.
